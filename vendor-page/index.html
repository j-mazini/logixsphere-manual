01

Overview: what the driver profile is for

The Driver / Vendor profile is made for subcontractors who need to follow their own work, performance, earnings, deductions, payslips, and requests. It is a personal account, not an administrative account.

Main use

Mobile self-service

Drivers mainly use the system from a phone to check their own information.

Access rule

Own data only

Every screen is filtered so the driver sees only records linked to their login.

Not admin

No management access

Drivers do not manage users, operations, invoices, deductions, or approvals.

Performance

Drivers can review daily and monthly performance, including route results, stops, operational indicators, punctuality notes, and verified operation status.

Personal metrics

Operations and earnings

Drivers can check worked days, completed routes, paid and unpaid stops, hours, calculated values, extras, route sort, and adhoc sort when available.

Own operations

Deductions and payslips

Drivers can view deductions assigned to them, open payslip details, check values, and download the payment document as a PDF.

Payment records

Requests

Drivers can submit requests, such as day off, holiday, and pre-payment, then follow the history and status. They cannot approve their own requests.

Submit and track

Simple rule

The driver profile is for checking personal work and payment information. It does not give access to company administration or other drivers' data.

02

Screens available to drivers

The driver has access to a limited set of screens. If a driver tries to open a screen that is not allowed, the system redirects them back to Daily Performance Insight.

Daily Performance Insight

The main driver screen. It shows daily route performance, stops, status, punctuality, notes, and indicators such as SPR and SPOR-H.

Current Performance

Shows monthly performance, averages, daily comparisons, stops, and operational indicators so the driver can follow progress over the month.

Current Month

Shows the current or selected month's operations and earnings, including worked days, routes, hours, paid stops, unpaid stops, and calculated values.

Subcontractor

Gives a more detailed operational view of the driver's own records, such as route, vehicle when available, stops, worked hours, values, notes, and status.

Deductions

Shows deductions linked to the driver, such as repairs, fines, advances, liquidation damages, or other administrative discounts.

Mobile Invoice

Also known as My Payslips. The driver can view payslips or invoices, open the details, check values, and download the PDF.

Requests

Lets the driver submit requests and follow their status history. Approval is handled by the appropriate administrative team.

03

What the driver can do

The driver can consult personal work records and payment-related documents. The profile is designed for visibility and follow-up, not for editing the operation.

Review daily performance

Drivers can check the route completed, operation status, number of stops, performance indicators, punctuality information, operation notes, and whether the operation was verified.

Daily insight

Follow monthly progress

Drivers can view performance averages, day-by-day results, stops, operational indicators, and comparisons between worked days.

Current performance

Check operations and earnings

Drivers can review worked days, routes, operation details, paid and unpaid stops, schedules, calculated values, extras, route sort, and adhoc sort when present.

Current month

View deductions and payslips

Drivers can consult deductions assigned to them and access payment documents. They can view details, confirm values, and download payslips as PDF files.

Payment details
04

Requests drivers can submit

Drivers can open requests for review and follow their history. Submitting a request does not mean it is approved; approval is handled outside the driver profile.

Day Off

A request for a specific day off. The driver submits the date and can follow the status after it is sent.

Holiday

A request for a period of absence or vacation. The request remains in the driver's history so it can be tracked later.

Pre-Payment

A request for an advance payment. The driver can submit it and follow the status, but cannot approve or process the payment.

Status history

After a request is submitted, it stays in the request history. The driver can check the status, but cannot approve, reject, or delete requests.

05

What the driver cannot do

The driver profile does not include administrative, operational editing, approval, or company finance permissions. It is intentionally limited to the driver's own records.

No administrative screens

Drivers cannot access the administrative Home, Week Planner, Daily Game Plan, Daily Operations Management, master records, contracts, assets, or consolidated reports.

No admin access

No operation editing

Drivers cannot create, change, finish, or approve operations. They also cannot register vendors, register vehicles, or change operational records.

Read-only operation

No company finance access

Drivers cannot access administrative finance, create invoices, create deductions, edit deductions, or manage company-level payment records.

Personal finance only

No access to other drivers

Drivers cannot view another driver's performance, deductions, payslips, requests, or personal information.

Own data only
06

How driver data is protected

The system identifies the driver through the login token. That token tells the system who the user is, which user type they have, which company they belong to, and which service partner is linked when applicable.

Allowed screens only

The driver sees only screens allowed for the Driver profile. If a restricted screen is opened, the system redirects the driver to Daily Performance Insight.

Filtered payslips and invoices

Payslips and invoices are filtered by the logged-in user. The system prevents one driver from opening another driver's payment document.

Filtered deductions

Deductions are filtered by the logged-in driver, so the driver only sees deductions linked to their own account.

Service partner link

When a service partner is linked to the token, service partner data follows that link. This keeps driver information connected to the correct account and company.
